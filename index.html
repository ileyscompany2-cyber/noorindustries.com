Mission & Vision

Professional Growth, Quality Products, and Trusted Services

Mission

  • To carry on business as producers and dealers in beverages and cosmetics.
  • To play an essential role in the economic development and social progress of Somalia.
  • To create economic opportunities and improve livelihoods.
  • To provide efficient and excellent beverage and cosmetics services.

Vision

  • To be a respected and trusted company delivering beyond expectations.
  • To develop strong management systems, leadership, and growth strategies.
  • To expand operations and optimize the use of human and economic resources.
  • To lead in the production and export of quality beverages and cosmetics in the region.
