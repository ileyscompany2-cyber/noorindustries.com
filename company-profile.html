Company Overview

NOOR INDUSTRIAL LIMITED

NOOR INDUSTRIAL LIMITED is a leading diversified manufacturing and industrial group based in Somalia, committed to producing high-quality Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) for local and international markets. The company specializes in manufacturing, packaging, importing, exporting, distributing, and supplying a wide range of consumer products while maintaining international standards of quality, innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
